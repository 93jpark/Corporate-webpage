    THE COMPNAY

    THE COMPANY is a full-service catering company based in Detroit, New York, San Francisco, and Seattle. Servicing weddings and event throughout multiple cities and the surrounding areas. The team of culinary experts seek to deliver a delicious and memorable cuisine experience.

    This company serves premium beverages that are tailored to their clients. These can be specialty crafted drinks to those that are sourced from local ingredients.

    San Francisco

    San Francisco, city and port, coextensive with San Francisco county, northern California, U.S., located on a peninsula between the Pacific Ocean and San Francisco Bay. It is a cultural and financial centre of the western United States and one of the country's most cosmopolitan cities.

    Seattle

    Seattle is the northernmost large city in the United States. The city is situated on an isthmus between Puget Sound (an inlet of the Pacific Ocean) and Lake Washington, about 100 miles (160 km) south of the Canada–United States border.
